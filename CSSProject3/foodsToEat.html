Adobo

Adobo: Considered by many as the national dish, this is a savory and tangy dish made with meat (often chicken or pork) slow-cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and black peppercorns.

Sinigang

Sinigang: A sour and savory soup that is often made with a tamarind broth and a variety of vegetables and meats like pork or fish.

Lechon

Lechon: A whole roasted pig, often served at special occasions and known for its crispy skin and tender meat.

Sisig

Sisig: A popular, sizzling dish made from chopped pork face, often with onions, chili peppers, and calamansi, and frequently topped with an egg.

Halo-halo

Halo-Halo: A popular and refreshing dessert that means "mix-mix," featuring a mix of shaved ice, milk, sweet beans, jellies, fruits, and often topped with leche flan, ube ice cream, or sweetened saba banana.
