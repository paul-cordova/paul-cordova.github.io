El Nido, Palawan

Known for its stunning limestone cliffs, crystal-clear waters, and white-sand beaches, El Nido is famous for island hoping tours that explore places like the Big Lagoona and Secret Lagoon.

Boracay

This island is world-renowned for its powdery white sand and beautiful sunsets, particularly at White Beach.

Bohol

Home to the unique Chocolate Hills, a geological formation of over 1,200 cone-shaped hills that turn brown during the dry season. It's also a place to see the adorable tarsiers.

Siargao

known as the "Surfing Capital of the Philippines," the island is famous for the cloud 9 surf break, but it also offers beautiful lagoons, rock pools, and a laid-back vibe.

Intramuros

This historic walled city is the oldest district in Manila, offering a glimpse into the country's Spanish colonial past with its cobblestone streets, old churches like the San Agustin Church, and museums.
